Pilau beef

Ingredients
  • 3 cups of rice
  • cooking oil
  • 2 onions
  • 1/4 kg beef meat(previously boiled)
  • ginger&garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon pilau masala
  • freshly cut yellow and green pepper
  • salt
  • corriander(dhania)
  • 3 tomatoes

Cooking steps

  1. In a cooking pot add some cooking oil and fry the cut onions then add the ginger & garlic paste and stir.Fry till golden brown .

  2. Add the previously boiled meat and fry until the meat turns golden brown.

  3. Then add tomatoes and the yellow &green bell pepper and stir.

  4. Then add the spices(paprika &p ilau masala) and stir until all ingredients are well stuffed together.

  5. Add salt to taste and cover to cook until you obtain a pastey look.

  6. Add water then add the rice and stir. Add salt to taste.

  7. Cover to cook and use medium heat to cook.

  8. When the water simmers down ,garnish with corriander.

  9. Stir and serve your ready pilau.
